The Desserts

Kutsinta

A type of steamed rice cake made from a mixture of rice flour, brown sugar and lye. Steamed in small ramekins. Our cooked cakes are topped with cheese

Maja Blanca

Thick, sweet coconut-flavored pudding with sweet kernels of corn. It is served in different presentations. Ours is so thick and firm that it's served in slices, with shredded cheese on top.

Cassava Cake

A type of bibingka (traditional baked cakes), having its origins from adopting native recipes but using cassava instead of the traditional glutinous rice batter. It is also known more rarely as cassava bibingka.
